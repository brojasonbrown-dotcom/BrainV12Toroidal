[PHIL] The human desire for deep empathic connection is real and profound, but it is mediated by social behavior, language, mirror neurons, and shared experience—not by quantum fields. This document presents the Psi-Linked concept as a philosophical provocation while honestly assessing its relationship to physics, neuroscience, and ethics.

**Important:** This is a conceptual technology assessment, not a scientific claim. No telepathic or remote communication capability is asserted.

[DISPROV] The psi-link concept violates all of these constraints. It proposes a field-mediated, faster-than-light, decoherence-free transfer of emotional states that can be cloned across multiple participants. This is not quantum mechanics; it is a fantasy that borrows quantum terminology.
