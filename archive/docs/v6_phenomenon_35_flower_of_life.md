The conflation of 6-fold and 60-fold symmetry is a basic mathematical error that undermines the credibility of the RHUFT framework. [DISPROV] The claim that the Flower of Life is the "actual structure of the vacuum" is pseudoscientific. The quantum vacuum in QFT is Lorentz-invariant and has no preferred spatial structure. If the vacuum had a hexagonal lattice structure, these calculations would be different, and the predictions would not match the experimental data. The claim that the Flower of Life describes the vacuum is not supported by any QFT calculation and is contradicted by the empirical success of QFT.

[EST PHYS] The RHUFT interpretation of the Flower of Life does not make any new predictions. The RHUFT interpretation risks the "Texas sharpshooter fallacy": drawing a target around a cluster of arrows. The pattern is common because it is simple, not because it is fundamental.

[PHIL] RHUFT observes that the Flower of Life is the fundamental geometric unit of this division system.
