## 1. Epistemology of Measurement

### 1.1 How Do We Know Our Equipment Measures Accurately?

[EST PHYS] The double-slit experiment has been performed with increasing precision over two centuries. Young's original experiment used sunlight split by a card, producing interference fringes on a screen. Modern variants use: electron guns (Davisson-Germer 1927, Tonomura 1989 with single-electron buildup), neutron interferometry (Rauch et al., 1974), and matter-wave interferometry with large molecules (C₆₀ buckyballs, Arndt et al., 1999; molecules up to ~2,000 amu, Eibenberger et al., 2013). [OBSERVED] Fringe visibilities exceeding 90% confirm the wave nature of matter. [EST PHYS] The calibration chain runs from macroscopic length standards (meter defined via c and the Cs hyperfine transition, Δν = 9,192,631,770 Hz) through interferometric displacement measurement, to electron wavelength calibrated via accelerating voltage (λ = h/√(2meV)).

[PHIL] Yet every measurement presupposes a theory. We "know" electrons are waves because we interpret fringe patterns through wave optics—but this is theory-laden observation. The fringe spacing Δx = λL/d is derived from wave theory; without that framework, the data are merely bright and dark bands. [HIST] This theory-ladenness was central to Kuhn's analysis of scientific revolutions and to Feyerabend's epistemological anarchism. We do not observe raw phenomena; we observe phenomena-as-interpreted.

[EST PHYS] In quantum mechanics, the measurement problem remains unresolved: no universally accepted derivation of the Born rule (probability = |ψ|²) exists from unitary Schrödinger evolution alone. [PHIL] This is the gap RHUFT addresses—not by filling it with new physics, but by reframing it as a question of geometric boundaries.

[PHIL] In the double-slit experiment, the wavefunction passing through two slits creates a superposition—a state of maximum geometric openness. [EST PHYS] The interference pattern arises from the phase difference Δφ = 2πd sinθ/λ between paths, where d is slit separation and λ is de Broglie wavelength. [MATH FACT] The trigonometric functions (sin, cos) are themselves built from incommensurable frequencies—the Taylor series of e^(iθ) involves all integer powers, and the zeros of sin are at π, 2π, 3π...—an irrational spacing that never repeats. [PHIL] This mathematical incommensurability mirrors the physical incommensurability: the two paths are never in phase at all points, creating the alternating constructive and destructive interference pattern.

[EST PHYS] When a which-path detector is introduced, it couples to one degree of freedom (position, spin, or internal state) of the particle. [EST PHYS] Decoherence theory (Zurek, 2003) shows that this coupling entangles the particle with the detector's environment, creating a mixed state ρ = Σᵢ |ψᵢ⟩⟨ψᵢ| that has no off-diagonal coherence terms in the which-path basis. [PHIL] The geometric memory term (Term II) is critical: the detector has a state that persists over time. Its memory (κ coupling) prevents the two paths from recombining coherently. [RHUFT AXIOM] The coherence function C(t) = |⟨Ψ(t)|Ψ(t−φτ)⟩|², where τ is a characteristic time scale, drops below the threshold λ = 1/φ² ≈ 0.381966 when the detector is present. [DISPROV] No such prediction exists. RHUFT currently retrodicts known phenomena; it does not predict new ones.

### 7.2 What Would Disprove It?

[DISPROV] The RHUFT geometric interpretation is already disproven as a *physical theory* in the following sense: it makes no predictions that differ from standard quantum mechanics. [PHIL] This is not a fatal flaw; the Copenhagen interpretation, many-worlds, and Bohmian mechanics are also experimentally indistinguishable. [OPEN] This is a genuine possibility. Current quantum gravity research does not use RHUFT's framework.

[DISPROV] If the Koide formula or similar empirical mass relations were shown to arise from standard QCD (e.g., through renormalization group dynamics), the geometric motivation for RHUFT would weaken. [EST PHYS] The Koide formula is already considered a coincidence by most physicists; its status does not affect the double-slit interpretation directly.

### 7.3 Current Assessment

[PHIL] The RHUFT interpretation of the double-slit experiment is a *coherent philosophical model* that is consistent with all known experimental data but adds no predictive power.
